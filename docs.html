Up and Going

Introduction

Nike Networth Calculator is an online software tool that helps users determine their net worth by taking several inputs of their Assets and Liabilities and computing to give the user accurate results.

Prerequisites: As a user, you are expected to have detailed data of your assets (what you own) and your liabilities (what you owe).

Main Features

  • Ease of Use: The Nike Networth Calculator is a simple app with obvious inputs fields that is both easy to use and get acquainted with. The User Experience designed is tailored to guide you effortlessly.
  • User Interface: Nike Networth Calculator offers a wonderful user interface. Employing a blue themed color scheme that is both welcoming and relaxing and clean and clear San Serif font, the application offers a great User Interface.
  • Responsiveness: Irrespective of your device, the Nike Networth Calculator is for you. Developed with cross-platform users in mind, the application is very responsive.

Future Features

  • You can now Sign Up with your favorite accounts (Google, Facebook)
  • Print Networth Data
  • See net-worth ranking amongst your friends

Why You Should Use

Whether you are a businessman of a salary earner, keeping track of your money is crucial, lest you make disastrous financial mistakes. The applications will help you know when you are falling off so you can make better decisions.

Quick Walkthrough: If you don't have an account, sign up, else sign in. You can Sign Up with Google or Facebook (This is a new feature). Upon sign in, you will be served to a dashboard where your calculations can be made. You can logout at the top right corner of the page to keep your data secure.
